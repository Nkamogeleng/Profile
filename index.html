About Me

Awarded third in the 2020 Tshwane Inter-university Challenge, Transport Category, second in the 2019 Unisa Research Awards, Nkamogeleng Matloga is a certified data Scientist, an award winning innovator and an entrepreneur. Currently in Software Development track in the Women Techsters Fellowship. She's the founder of BCycle SA and Hunadi Cycle Projects. The South African role model has been invited to speak to organisations across South Africa including the Unisa Business counsel, Thobela FM, Pretoria News, Green Africa, Women in Tech and Rekord News paper. In her free time when she's not Working, at school or running her business, she loves travelling. She finds peace in nature and joy in meeting new people. Additionally, Nkamogeleng is very passionate about the youth and equality. She has a passion for solving normalised sociatal issues like unemployement and women discrimination.
